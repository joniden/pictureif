PICTURE IF

In the current climate of the Syrian refugee crisis - there is a vast amount of supporters wanting to contribute but not knowing where to start. This has lead to a lot people failing to join in the battle.

We aim to reach out to Scandinavians in the hope of spreading awareness and spark engagement by facilitating information regarding the refugee crisis and linking organizations, advocating different areas where support is needed. In a few steps we provide options on how you as an individual may contribute or take action by directing you to organizations you wish to support.
